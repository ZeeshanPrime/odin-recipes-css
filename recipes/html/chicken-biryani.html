Chicken Biryani

Description

A fragrant, spiced rice dish layered with marinated chicken, chicken biryani is a South Asian classic. It blends basmati rice, aromatic whole spices, and a rich yogurt-based masala to create deep flavor in every bite. Traditionally slow-cooked on low heat, the steam locks in flavor and tenderness. Whether served at weddings or Sunday family dinners, chicken biryani is a full meal that hits every taste note: spicy, savory, and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Biryani masala
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh coriander & mint
  • Lemon juice
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger garlic paste for 1 hour.
  2. Fry onions until golden; add tomatoes and cook into a masala.
  3. Add marinated chicken, cook until tender.
  4. In a separate pot, boil rice with whole spices until 80% done.
  5. Layer chicken and rice in a pot with herbs, chilies, and lemon.
  6. Cover and steam on low heat (“dum”) for 15–20 minutes.
  7. Mix gently before serving.
