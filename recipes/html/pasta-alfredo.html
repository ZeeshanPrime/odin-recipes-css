Pasta Alfredo

Description

Pasta Alfredo is a classic Italian dish known for its rich, creamy texture and simple, elegant flavor. Made with butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese, it delivers a smooth, velvety sauce that perfectly coats each strand of pasta. Often served with fettuccine, it's a go-to comfort food that feels both luxurious and easy to prepare. Ideal for both weeknight dinners and special occasions, Pasta Alfredo is loved for its satisfying taste and timeless appeal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (fettuccine preferred)
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Heavy cream
  • Cloves (minced)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in cream, stir and let it simmer on low heat.
  4. Add Parmesan, mix until the sauce is smooth and thick.
  5. Add cooked pasta, stir until fully coated.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
